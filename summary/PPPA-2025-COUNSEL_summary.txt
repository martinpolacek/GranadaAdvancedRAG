**I. Call for Proposals (PPPA-2025-COUNSEL):**

**1. WHO:**

*   **Eligible Applicants:**
    *   Legal entities (public or private bodies)

    *   Specific entity types: Social partner organisations, Public authorities, Non-profit organisations, Research centers/institutes, Higher Education Institutions (HEIs), Civil Society Organisations (CSOs)

**2. WHAT:**

*   **Type:** EU Action Grant (Pilot Project and Preparatory Action - PPPA).
*   **Focus:** "Preparatory work for counselling structures to support mobile migrant labour." The action aims to develop the knowledge base about counseling networks and its impact on fair mobility by third-country nationals posted and working temporarily in the EU.

**3. HOW:**

*   **Submission Procedure:** Electronic submission through the EU Funding & Tenders Portal Electronic Submission System.
*   **Required Documents:**
    *   Application Form Part A (administrative information, online)
    *   Application Form Part B (technical description, using provided template, max 50 pages).
    *   CVs of core project team.
    *   Activity reports of last year.
    *   List of previous projects.
*   **Key Requirements for Participants:** Admissibility and eligibility (sections 5 and 6 of the document), confirm the mandate to act for all applicants, confirm the information in the application is correct and complete, compliance with the conditions for receiving EU funding, legal and financial set-up of the Grant Agreements (sections 5 and 10).

**4. WHY:**

*   **Objectives:**
    *   Research about mobile migrant labour and the impact assisting counselling structures can have on this target group.
    *   Setting up and running a trade union-related transnational counselling network for short-term mobile migrant workers (EU citizens and third-country nationals) in at least 7 countries
*   **Expected Impact:** An evaluation of the existing counselling services for the target groups and a mapping of the (sector-specific) needs for counselling services should be developed. Based on the findings of the study, recommendations shall be formulated for the preparation of a possible dedicated initiative for the establishment of a European counselling network for mobile migrant labour
