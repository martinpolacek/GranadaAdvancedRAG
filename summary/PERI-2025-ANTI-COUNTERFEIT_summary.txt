Here's a comprehensive summary of the provided data regarding the Programme for the Protection of the Euro against Counterfeiting (PERICLES IV) call for proposals:

**1. WHO:**

*   **Organizer:** European Commission, Directorate-General for Economic and Financial Affairs (DG ECFIN). [May be managed by other EU bodies].
*   **Eligible Applicants:** Competent national authorities in the area of protection of the euro against counterfeiting from Euro area and non-Euro area Member States.

**2. WHAT:**

*   **Type:** Call for Proposals for EU Action Grants
*   **Focus:** Projects aimed at preventing and combating counterfeiting of the euro, preserving the integrity of euro banknotes and coins and strengthening trust in the Unionâ€™s economy.

**3. HOW:**

*   **Submission Procedure:** Proposals must be submitted electronically via the Funding & Tenders Portal Electronic Submission System. Paper submissions are not allowed.
*   **Application Form:**
    *   Part A: Administrative information and summarized budget (filled directly online).
    *   Part B: Technical description of the project (download template, complete, upload as PDF; limited to 70 pages).
*   **Mandatory Annexes:**
    *   Detailed budget table.
    *   Draft agenda/outline (for events or studies, if any).
*   **Key Requirements:**
    *   Applicants must register in the Participant Register and obtain a PIC (Participant Identification Code).
    *   The online summarized budget table must correspond with the amounts in the detailed budget table.

**4. WHY:**

*   **Objectives:**
    *   Support and supplement Member State measures against counterfeiting.
    *   Assist national authorities in developing cooperation and exchanging best practices.
*   **Expected Results:**
    *   Improved cooperation among Member States affected by counterfeiting.
    *   Enhanced cooperation with third countries where counterfeit euro production is suspected.
    *   An efficient framework for the protection of the euro in South Eastern Europe.
    *   Countering distribution of counterfeits online, increased cooperation with customs and judiciary.

**5. HOW MUCH:**

*   **Total Budget:** EUR 600,000
*   **Form of Grant:** Budget-based mixed actual cost grant (actual costs, with unit cost and flat-rate elements).
*   **Funding Rate:** 75% of eligible costs (possibility of 90% for priority actions)

**6. OTHER IMPORTANT INFORMATION:**

*   **Deadlines:** Two cut-off dates: April 23, 2025, and September 16, 2025, at 17:00 CET (Brussels).
*   **Project Implementation:** Projects must start after October 15, 2025, (1st cut-off) and January 1, 2026 (2nd cut-off).
*   **Key Activities:** Exchange of information, technical/scientific/operational assistance, purchase of equipment in third countries (limited).
*   **VAT:** Non-deductible/non-refundable VAT is eligible, except for VAT paid by public bodies acting as public authority.
*   **No-Profit Rule:** Grants may NOT produce a profit.
*   **Specific Requirements for Projects**: Must follow the highest ethical standards.
*   **Reporting:** Reporting must be in the language of the Agreement.
*   **Confidentiality:** The beneficiary must take measures to ensure data protection.
*   **Access Rights:** The granting authority has the right to use non-sensitive data for policy information, communication, dissemination and publicity purposes.
*   **In-kind contributions:** In-kind contributions for free are allowed, but cost-neutral.

This summary should provide a solid understanding of the PERICLES IV call for proposals. Always refer to the original call documents for the most accurate and detailed information.
