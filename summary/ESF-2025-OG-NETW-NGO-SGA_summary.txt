**Project Summary: Annual Operating Grants for EU Level Social NGO Networks**

**1. WHO:**

*   **Organiser:** European Commission, Directorate-General for Employment, Social Affairs and Inclusion (DG EMPL), managing the Employment and Social Innovation (EaSI) strand of the ESF+.
*   **Eligible Applicants:** EU-level networks of civil society organizations
    *   Legal entities (public or private bodies)
    *   Established in eligible countries (EU Member States, listed EEA countries, and countries associated to the ESF+)
    *   Non-governmental, non-profit-making, independent of conflicting interests.
    *   Have statutory aims falling under the call's objectives.
    *   Have national member organisations in at least 14 EU Member States.
    *   Have mainly non-profit members.
    *   Be mandated to represent members at the EU level.
    *   Organisations that have first been awarded a framework partnership agreement (FPA) under call ESF-2025-OG-NETW-NGO-FPA.

**2. WHAT:**

*   **Type of Program:** Call for proposals for EU annual operating grants.
*   **Focus:** Supporting EU-level Social NGO Networks to promote social inclusion, combat poverty, implement the European Pillar of Social Rights (EPSR), and engage with the European Semester process. Activities include policy advice, analysis, training, mutual learning, awareness-raising, and dissemination.

**3. HOW:**

*   **Submission Procedure:**
    *   Proposals must be submitted electronically via the Funding & Tenders Portal Electronic Submission System. Paper submissions are not accepted.
    *   A 2-step process is required: creating a user account & registering the organization and submitting the proposal.
    *   The submission includes Part A (administrative data online), Part B (technical description as PDF - max 40 pages), and mandatory annexes (CVs, list of previous projects).
*   **Key Requirements:** Adherence to page limits, use of provided templates, and demonstration of the organization's capacity. Mandatory templates: CVs of core project team, List of previous projects.

**4. WHY:**

*   **Objectives:**
    *   Promote social inclusion and combat poverty in line with the European Pillar of Social Rights.
    *   Support the implementation of Commission's EU policy initiatives.
    *   Support the monitoring of relevant national and regional/local level actions undertaken as part of implementation of the EU-level instruments set out in the EPSR.
    *   Strengthen the capacity of EU-level networks and their members.
*   **Expected Results:**
    *   Improved awareness of EU policies (EPSR).
    *   Increased awareness among EU and national authorities.
    *   Increased capacity of networks to support policy-making.
    *   Enhanced research and data availability.
    *   Increased awareness of national, regional and local member organizations.

**5. HOW MUCH:**

*   **Call Budget:** EUR 13,250,000 total, with EUR 500,000 specifically for capacity building of social service providers.
*   **Operating Grant Range:** Indicatively between EUR 100,000 and EUR 1,250,000 per work programme.
*   **Funding Rate:** 85% of eligible costs.
*   **Form of Grant:** budget-based mixed actual cost grant.
